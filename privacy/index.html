Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

At Ovelo, we take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

2. Our Privacy Principles

Ovelo is designed with privacy as a core principle. Our application is:

  • Local-First: All data is stored locally on your computer, not in the cloud.
  • Content-Blind: We do not record WHAT you type, only HOW you work (typing rhythm, mouse activity, app switches).
  • No Screenshots: We never take screenshots of your screen.
  • No Keylogging: We do not record the specific keys you press.

3. What Data We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following data:

  • Activity Patterns: Information about app switches, typing rhythm, and mouse activity.
  • Timestamps: When certain activities occur.
  • App Names: Names of active applications (not the content).

4. How We Use Data

Your data is used to:

  • Generate the Day Trace visualization.
  • Create AI reflections about your productivity.
  • Identify patterns in your focus.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored in a local database on your computer. We do not send personal data to external servers, except:

  • AI Reflections: To generate reflections, anonymized data is sent to our AI provider. This data contains no personally identifiable information.
  • Payment Processing: If you subscribe to Pro, payment information is processed by our payment provider (Stripe).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and delete your local data.
  • Uninstall the application at any time, which will delete all local data.
  • Temporarily pause tracking via the application settings.

7. Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data. Since data is stored locally, security also depends on the security of your own computer.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the application or by email (if applicable).

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@ovelolab.com.
